Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

Welcome to Brig Radio, the official campus communication platform for Brilliant Grammar School Educational Society’s Group of Institutions (BRIG). Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our student community:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, student ID, college email, and department.
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how you interact with the radio sessions, news feeds, and placement hub.
  • Notifications: We store your preferences for push notifications and email alerts.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing official campus announcements and live radio broadcasts.
  • Organizing and tracking placement opportunities.
  • Processing and responding to student suggestions.
  • Maintaining platform security and preventing misinformation.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared within the BRIG administration for official academic and administrative purposes.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information through your account settings or by contacting the campus administration.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at the BRIG campus office.
